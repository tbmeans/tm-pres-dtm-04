Discovery of Extrasolar Planets


There are a total of 108 discovered extrasolar planetary systems.

A detection of three planets in one extrasolar system by radial velocity has occurred only twice, including the 55 Cancri system.

Resonance:  the existence of small integer ratios between orbital frequencies

Resonances affect the timing and strength of gravitational perturbations by bodies other than the parent star

Orbital modeling of the radial velocity detection data suggest a 3:1 resonance between the two inner planets.
