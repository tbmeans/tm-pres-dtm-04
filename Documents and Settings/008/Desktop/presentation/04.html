Recent Studies of the extrasolar planetary orbits in 55 Cancri



Previous Numerical Simulations over 1 Million year timescales

Zhou et al.
400 simulations, 133 unstable.  Found evidence for 3:1 resonance


Ji et al.  
Did not find evidence that the 3:1 resonance exists over a long time period




Theoretical explanations

Novak et al suggest that the two planets began in resonance farther away,
and as they migrated inward to their current position,
 tidal forces on 55 Cancri b by the host star eliminated the resonance.
